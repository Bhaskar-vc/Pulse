CARD

Card

Cards are flexible containers that group related content and actions. Use them to surface information in a scannable, structured layout.

Stable Accessible Figma Ready

Overview

The default card has a white background, subtle border, and 12px border-radius. It lifts on hover via a soft shadow. Cards adapt to any content — text, images, stats, avatars — and can be interactive (clickable) or static.

Accessibility

Interactive cards

  • Use a <button> or <a> as the root element when the entire card is clickable
  • Provide a descriptive aria-label if the visible label is ambiguous
  • Ensure focus ring is visible — do not suppress outline

Static cards

  • Use semantic HTML inside: headings, lists, paragraphs
  • Card images should have descriptive alt text
  • Decorative images should use alt=""

Selection cards

  • Use role="radio" or role="checkbox" with aria-checked for selectable cards
  • Group them with role="radiogroup" or role="group"

Card grids

  • Use a meaningful <ul>/<li> structure for card lists
  • Provide a region label via aria-label on the grid container
