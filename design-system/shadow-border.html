Shadow & Border

Elevation shadows, border colors, border-radius values, and focus rings — the foundational tokens that define depth, containment, and interactive states across the system.

Stable Figma Ready

Shadows

Shadows convey elevation. Use lighter shadows for cards and tooltips, heavier ones for modals and floating panels. All shadows use #10182808 / #10182814 (black at 3% / 8% opacity) for a neutral tone that works on any background.
